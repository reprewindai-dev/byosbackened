18 U.S.C. §2257 Compliance Statement

Last updated: January 1, 2026
Important: GoonVault is a content aggregation platform. The vast majority of content displayed on this site is embedded from third-party platforms via their official embed APIs. GoonVault does not produce, host, or distribute video content — it merely displays embeds from source platforms.

Embedded Third-Party Content

All video content embedded from third-party platforms (including but not limited to PornHub, XHamster, RedTube, and LustPress) is subject to the §2257 compliance statements and record-keeping of those respective platforms. GoonVault is not the primary producer of such content.

Users seeking to verify age and consent documentation for embedded content should contact the originating platform directly.

Creator-Uploaded Original Content

For any original content uploaded directly to GoonVault by creators:

  • All performers must be 18 years of age or older at the time of production
  • Creators are required to maintain records as required by 18 U.S.C. §2257 and 28 C.F.R. Part 75
  • Creators must submit age verification and consent documentation upon upload
  • GoonVault maintains copies of submitted documentation as the secondary producer

Exemption Statement

With respect to content that was not produced by GoonVault, GoonVault is exempt from the record-keeping requirements of 18 U.S.C. §2257 pursuant to 28 C.F.R. §75.7, as GoonVault is a secondary producer that does not own or otherwise possess the originals of the visual depictions.

Zero Tolerance for Underage Content

GoonVault has a strict zero-tolerance policy for any content involving individuals under the age of 18. Any such content will be immediately removed and reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement. Users who upload, share, or attempt to access such content will be permanently banned and their information reported to authorities.

To report suspected underage content: contact us immediately via the platform.
