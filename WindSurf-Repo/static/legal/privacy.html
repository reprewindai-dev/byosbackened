GoonVault

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

GoonVault is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service. We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and password (hashed) if you create an account
  • Subscription data: Billing information is processed and stored by Stripe — we never store your full card details
  • Usage data: Pages visited and search queries (stored anonymously, no cross-session tracking)
  • Age verification: A browser cookie confirming you have confirmed your age (no ID collected)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your viewing history across sessions
  • We do not build behavioral profiles
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers
  • We do not share your email with any third party except Stripe for billing

4. Cookies

We use a single first-party cookie to remember your age verification confirmation. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

5. Third-Party Embeds

Videos are embedded from third-party platforms (PornHub, XHamster, etc.) via their official embed codes. When you play a video, you are loading content directly from those platforms — their own privacy policies apply to that connection. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those platforms.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Stripe retains billing records per their own data retention policies.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. We employ security measures including rate limiting, intrusion detection, and zero-trust request verification.

8. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to deletion of your account and data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the platform.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email.
