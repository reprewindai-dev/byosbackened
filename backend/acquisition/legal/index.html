All the documents your procurement team needs.

These documents are provided as the public-facing standing terms. Specific commercial agreements signed with Enterprise customers may amend or supersede portions of these documents under their executed Master Services Agreement.

A.Documents available under NDA

The following are not posted publicly. They are negotiated as part of a commercial engagement after a written technical evaluation and an executed mutual non-disclosure agreement.

  • Master Services Agreement (MSA) — the master commercial contract, including indemnification, audit rights, change control, and acceptance criteria. Standard procurement redlines accepted under the Sovereign · Enterprise tier.
  • Data Processing Agreement (DPA) — required for GDPR, CPRA, LGPD-aligned engagements. Customer is the Controller; Veklom is not a Processor for self-hosted deployments because no customer data flows to Veklom.
  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA) — for HIPAA-covered entities. Veklom is not a Business Associate for self-hosted deployments because no PHI flows to Veklom; we will sign a BAA for clarity if your covered-entity policy requires it.
  • Statement of Work (SOW) — for engagements that include implementation assistance, custom feature commitments, or compliance-evidence packages.
  • Reseller / White-Label Agreement — for Sovereign · Pro and Enterprise customers exercising their white-label rights.
