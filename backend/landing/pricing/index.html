What is an Operating Reserve?

A USD-denominated balance used for governed execution, exports, and evidence work. It makes budget control explicit before AI runs.

Can regulated teams bring their own model keys?

Yes. BYOK governance calls let teams keep provider ownership while Veklom controls routing, policy, audit, and spend checks.

Is evidence access included?

Audit records are always created. Signed exports, evidence packages, and auditor bundles are paid artifacts because they carry procurement and compliance value.

Do reserves expire?

Reserve balances are USD-denominated and do not expire under the public pricing model shown here.
