Frequently asked questions

What's a "credit"?

One credit = one LLM request through the gateway. Credits are consumed based on actual usage. Unused credits don't roll over.

Can I self-host Veklom?

Yes. Veklom is designed to run in your infrastructure — on-premise, VPC, or air-gapped. We don't hold your API keys or data.

What's the yearly discount?

Pay for 10 months, get 12. That's 17% off compared to monthly billing. Billed annually upfront.

Do you offer pilots?

Yes. Contact sales for a 30-day pilot with dedicated onboarding support. Pilot pricing is customized based on scope.

What payment methods?

Credit card for self-serve. Enterprise customers typically pay via wire/ACH with NET 30 terms.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Contact support for mid-cycle adjustments.
